Municipalities of Bulacan

Bulacan consists of various municipalities, each with its own rich history, culture, and attractions. Here are some notable municipalities in the province:

Malolos

The capital city of Bulacan and home to the historic Barasoain Church.

Meycauayan

Known as an industrial hub and famous for its jewelry-making industry.

Baliuag

Famous for its Semana Santa procession and local crafts.

Pulilan

Home of the famous Carabao Festival celebrated every May.

San Miguel

Known for Biak-na-Bato National Park, a historical and natural site.

Marilao

A rapidly growing urban municipality known for St. Michael the Archangel Parish.
